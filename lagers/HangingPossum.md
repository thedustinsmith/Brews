# Hanging Possum

Recipe is for 6 gallons

* 8lbs 2 row malt
* .5lb aromatic malt (vienna)
* 1.5lb flaked corn
* 1.5oz German Saphir Hops @ 60
* 1oz Hallertau @ 60
* 1oz Hallertau @ 20
* .5oz Halltertau @ 10
* .5oz German Saphir @ 2
* .25tsp irish moss
* Lager Yeast (WLP800 / WLP810)

OG: 1.046-1.050
FG: 1.012-1.014
B: 31BU
SRM: 6
Alcohol: 4.5%
